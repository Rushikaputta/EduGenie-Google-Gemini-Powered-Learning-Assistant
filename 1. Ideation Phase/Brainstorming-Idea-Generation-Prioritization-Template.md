# Brainstorming – Idea Generation & Prioritization Template
**Project:** EduGenie – Google Gemini Powered Learning Assistant

---

## 1. Problem Space

> What problem are we solving?

Students struggle to:
- Understand complex topics without a teacher
- Self-assess their knowledge
- Find structured, personalized study plans
- Get instant answers to doubts outside classroom hours
