# Define Problem Statements Template
**Project:** EduGenie – Google Gemini Powered Learning Assistant

---

## 1. Problem Statement (Formal)

> **"Students lack access to personalized, on-demand educational support that can adapt to their individual learning needs, explain complex concepts, assess their understanding, and guide them through a structured learning journey — outside of traditional classroom settings."**

---

## 2. Problem Breakdown

### Who is affected?
- School and college students (primary users)
- Self-learners and professionals upskilling
- Teachers looking for quick content generation tools

### What is the problem?
| Pain Point | Current Situation | Impact |
|------------|-------------------|--------|
| No instant doubt resolution | Students wait for teacher/tutor | Learning delays |
| No personalized learning plans | Generic textbook-based study | Low retention |
| No self-assessment tools | Manual quiz creation is time-consuming | Poor exam prep |
| Complex jargon | Hard to understand technical topics alone | Frustration, dropout |
| No topic summarization | Reading lengthy books takes hours | Time inefficiency |

### When does the problem occur?
- While studying at home without a tutor
- Before exams with no structured revision plan
- When encountering an unfamiliar concept in class

### Where does the problem occur?
- Home study sessions
- Online learning environments
- Self-paced courses without mentorship

### Why does the problem exist?
- AI tools for education are not easily accessible or user-friendly
- Existing tools (ChatGPT, etc.) require prompt engineering knowledge
- No single tool covers summarization + quiz + learning path + Q&A together

---

## 3. Root Cause Analysis (5 Whys)

| Why # | Question | Answer |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Why 1 | Why do students struggle to learn independently? | No personalized guidance available |
| Why 2 | Why is personalized guidance unavailable? | Tutors are expensive and limited |
| Why 3 | Why are AI tools not filling this gap? | Most AI tools are generic, not education-focused |
| Why 4 | Why aren't education-focused AI tools widely used? | Complex interfaces, lack of features |
| Why 5 | Why aren't developers building education-specific AI tools? | High development barrier with raw LLM APIs |

---

## 4. Proposed Solution Statement

> Build **EduGenie** — a simple, powerful web application powered by Google Gemini that gives every student access to a personalized AI tutor capable of summarizing topics, generating quizzes, creating learning paths, answering questions, and explaining concepts.
