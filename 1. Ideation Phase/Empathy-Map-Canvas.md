## Key Insights for EduGenie

1. Students need **immediate answers** — not wait for a teacher
2. They want **structure** — a clear path from beginner to advanced
3. **Self-testing** is critical — they know it but lack the tools
4. Language must be **simple** — not academic/jargon-heavy
5. The tool must be **fast and easy** — no complex setup
